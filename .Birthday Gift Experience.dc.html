PHASE 07 — THE LETTER
{{ recipientName }},

I started this letter four different ways and none of them sounded like us. So here’s the plain version.

This year I watched you do hard things quietly — hold people up without being asked, laugh anyway on days that didn’t deserve it. You think nobody notices. I notice everything.

I can’t fit a whole year of being lucky onto one page, so instead: thank you. For the coffee handed over without a word. For dancing badly in the kitchen, on purpose. For choosing us on every ordinary day like it’s nothing.

Whatever this next year turns out to be, I want the seat next to you.

happy birthday, my love.
— J.
